Ratan Tata

Ratan Tata is an Indian industrialist, philanthropist, and former chairman of Tata Sons, the holding company of the Tata Group. He was born on December 28, 1937, in Mumbai, India, to a prominent Parsi family with a legacy of business and philanthropy.Tata completed his education at Cornell University and Harvard Business School before joining the Tata Group in 1962. Under his leadership, the group expanded its businesses across various sectors, including steel, automobiles, software, telecommunications, and hospitality. Tata is widely recognized for his visionary leadership, ethical values, and commitment to social causes.
